This Fuel Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is made at the place and on the date mentioned in the schedule hereto (“Schedule”), amongst:


TATA MOTORS FINANCE SOLUTIONS LIMITED , a company within the meaning of the Companies Act, 2013 and a non-banking finance company registered under Chapter IIIB of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 and having its registered office at 14, 4th Floor, SIR H.C. Dinshaw Building 16, Horniman Circle, Fort, Mumbai 400 001 IN, Maharashtra, and a branch/office mentioned in the Schedule hereto (hereinafter referred to as the “Lender/TMFSL”, which expression shall, unless it be repugnant to the context, or meaning thereof, include its successors and assigns) of the FIRST PART ;

AND

The person(s) who are named in the Schedule hereto as Borrower and Co-Borrower(s), hereinafter collectively referred to as “Borrower” of the SECOND PART;

The Borrower and the Lender are hereinafter individually referred to as a “Party” and collectively as the “Parties”.

WHEREAS:


a. The Borrower has subscribed to the Fuel Programme offered by the Oil Company, wherein the Borrower is eligible to purchase automotive fuel/lubricants from the designated retail fuel outlets of the Oil Company, by using a prepaid fleet card (“Fuel Card”) issued by such Oil Company.
b. The Lender and the Oil Company have entered into an arrangement, where the Borrower, on fulfilling the credit criteria prescribed by the Lender, may avail a fuel loan from the Lender for the purchase of such automotive fuel/lubricant from the designated outlets of the Oil Company, which will loaded on the Fuel Card through the fuel card.
c. Thereby, at the request of the Borrower, the Lender has agreed to grant a loan facility upto a certain Limit (details whereof are mentioned in the Schedule I hereto) to the Borrower, inter alia for the purpose of purchase of automotive fuel and/or lubricants, as mentioned in the Schedule hereto, payable directly to the Oil Company in the manner and on the terms and conditions as hereinafter mentioned.

1. DEFINITIONS

“Availability Period” shall mean such period or the period up to such date, as mentioned in the Schedule hereto or as may be extended by the Lender in writing at its sole discretion.

“Accelerated Interest” shall mean such additional rate of interest, as more specifically mentioned in the Schedule hereto, that will be payable by the Borrower on the Outstanding Balance in the event if the Borrower does not pay the Payable Amount in full, on or before the Payment Due Date, and such Accelerated Interest will continue to be charged until the entire Payable Amount is repaid by the Borrower.

“Active Account” shall mean the Loan account, in respect of which the Payable Amount remains due and payable for a period from 0 DPD to 14 DPD.

“Billing Cycle” shall mean the 1st calendar day of a month and shall end on the last Business day of that month.

“Billing Date” shall mean 1st calendar Day or such other day, as may be decided by the Lender, of the month succeeding the Billing Cycle.

“Blocked Account” shall mean the Loan account, in respect of which the Payable Amount remains due and payable beyond 90 DPD.

“Business Day” shall mean the day on which the branches and offices of Lender as also the banks are open for business and excludes any Sunday and public holidays in such places.

“DRT Act” shall mean Recovery of Debts Due to Banks and Financial Institutions Act, 1993, the rules and regulations thereunder, and as may be amended, re-enacted, replaced, re-titled, from time to time.

“Conditions Precedent” shall mean such conditions as are more specifically mentioned in clause 2.4 of the Agreement.

“Cut-Off Limit” shall mean such limit which is equivalent to 95 % of the Limit or such percentage of the Limit as may be communicated by the Lender to the Borrower..

“DPD” shall mean days past due.

“Designated Account” shall mean the current account of Oil Company designated for the purpose of transferring the amounts payable by the Company in relation to the Loan, as more particularly mentioned in the Schedule hereto.

“Event(s) of Default” shall mean the events of default mentioned in Clause 7 hereunder and any events of default mentioned in any other documents pursuant to the Loan.

“Final Settlement Date” shall mean the date on which all the Outstanding Balance under this Agreement have been irrevocably paid/repaid by the Borrower to the Lender, to the full satisfaction of the Lender.

“Frozen Account” shall mean the Loan account, in respect of which the Payable Amount remains due and payable for a period from 15 DPD upto 90 DPD.

“Fuel Programme” shall mean the fuel/lubricant programme owned, managed and offered by the Oil Company, which enables the Borrower to purchase automotive fuel/lubricants from the designated retail fuel outlets of such Oil Company as per the terms and conditions agreed upon between the Borrower and the Oil Company.

“Fuel Card” shall mean the prepaid fleet card issued by the Oil Company to the Borrower, which shall be loaded or recharged by means of the Wallet maintained by the Borrower with the Fuel Company, so as to enable the purchase of fuel and/or lubricants by the Borrower from the designated retail fuel outlets of the Oil Company.

2. LOAN AMOUNT, DISBURSEMENTS AND CONDITIONS PRECEDENT:
2.1 The Borrower hereby agrees to avail from the Lender, and subject to the Borrower fulfilling and performing within the Availability Period the Conditions Precedent, the Lender hereby agrees to provide to the Borrower, the loan not exceeding the amount as more specifically mentioned in the Schedule hereto, in one or more tranches within the Cut-Off Limit(each, a “Tranche”, and all such Tranches, shall jointly or severally, be referred to as a “Loan”), for the Purpose, in the manner and upon and subject to the terms and conditions mentioned in this Agreement. This Loan shall be valid for a period of 365 (Three Hundred and Sixty Five) days and at the written request of the Borrower, can be renewed for such further period of 12 (twelve) months each. The decision for such renewal shall be at the sole discretion of the Lender.
2.2 Once the drawdown request is raised to the Lender by the Borrower or the Oil Company on behalf of the Borrower or any other person who may be so authorised, if the Loan amount so requested is within the Cut-off Limit (each, a Tranche), the amount of the Tranche shall be transferred by the Lender into the Designated Account of the Oil Company, and the Oil Company, in turn, shall then load the Tranche amount on to the Wallet of the Borrower and thereby recharge/load the Fuel Card of the Borrower. The Borrower hereby expressly and irrevocably authorises the Lender to disburse the Loan amount in favour of the Oil Company, in the manner mentioned hereinabove. The Borrower shall be permitted to raise draw down requests for Tranches in multiples of thousand only.
2.3 The following shall be the conditions precedent (“Conditions Precedent”): (a) The Borrower shall have duly signed, executed and delivered to the Lender or the Lender’s authorised agent, the Payment Instruments, resolutions, certificates, writings, consents, authorizations, approvals, affidavits, no-objections, declarations, undertakings, indemnity, any other documents/writings as may be required by the Lender; (b) The Borrower shall have delivered to the Lender or the Lender’s authorised agent any certificates, documents etc., as may be required by the Lender; (c) The Borrower shall have duly paid the fees and charges as applicable; and (d) The Borrower shall have duly fulfilled such other conditions as may be prescribed by the Lender.
2.4 The Loan shall be utilised by the Borrower solely for the Purpose and the Loan shall not be used for any other purpose or for any immoral, illegal, speculative, unauthorized purpose or activity. Without being obligated to do so, the Lender shall be entitled to monitor use/end use of the Loan including through any auditor(s) or consultant(s) including examining books of the Borrower, with necessary certification from them, as appointed by the Lender at its sole discretion and at the cost of the Borrower.
2.5 In the event the Loan is cancelled for any reason (any cancellation always being subject to the discretion of the Lender), the Borrower shall be liable to pay to the Lender the entire Outstanding Balance with cancellation charges as mentioned in the Schedule of Charges in the Schedule-A hereto.


3. REPAYMENT, INTEREST AND FEES :
3.1 Outstanding Balance shall mean, at any point in time, the aggregate of principal amount of the Tranche(s) that have been drawn down, together with all Interest, Accelerated Interest,, costs, expenses and all other moneys whatsoever, whether any of them are due or not at such point in time, but which remain payable, by the Borrower to the Lender under the terms of this Agreement.
3.2 Upon the Borrower availing of the first Tranche of the Loan, the Lender shall issue a Monthly Statement on the Billing Date for each Billing Cycle. The Monthly Statement shall be in a format provided for in Schedule B hereto, and shall include details pertaining to the Tranche(s) availed of by the Borrower, the Payable Amount in respect thereof, the Billing cycle, the Payment Due Date, the Interest rate or the Accelerated Interest, other charges, the status of the Loan account of the Borrower and such other particulars as provided for in the format.
3.3 The Borrower shall be liable to make the payment to the Lender of the Payable Amount specified in the Monthly Statement on or before the Payment Due Date.
3.4 In the event if the Borrower fails to make the due and complete repayment to the Lender of the Payable Amount on or before the Payment Due Date, the Borrower shall be liable to pay Accelerated Interest on the Outstanding Balance and such Accelerated Interest will continue to be charged by the Lender until the entire Payable Amount is repaid by the Borrower.
3.5 The Borrower shall be entitled to avail of and make draw down requests for further Tranches in respect of the Loan, up to the extent of the available Cut-off Limit, till such time that the Loan Account of the Borrower is an Active Account. If the Borrower continues to default in the repayment of the Payable Amount, and the Loan Account becomes a Frozen Account, the Borrower shall not be entitled to avail of any further Tranches in respect of the Loan, until such date the Borrower repays the entire Payable Amount alongwith the Accelerated Interest accrued thereon and the charges etc, and the Loan Account becomes an Active Account.
3.6 In case where the Loan Account of the Borrower continues to remain a Frozen Account beyond a period of 90 (ninety) DPD, the Loan Account shall become a Blocked Account.
3.7 The Borrower has perused, understood and agreed to Lender's method of determining the Interest Rate, method of application of the Interest Rate and Accelerated Interest as well as of calculating Payable Amount as also the appropriation thereof into principal and interest. The Borrower also acknowledges that the Interest Rate will be applicable on the daily running balance of the Borrower’s Loan account. An illustration of the methodology of charging of interest and the amounts payable is set out in the Schedule B hereto.
3.8 The Borrower may at any time payoff the entire Outstanding Balance arising out of this Loan and request the Lender to close the Loan account of the Borrower.
3.9 All Taxes in relation to any transaction pursuant to this Agreement, including interest tax, if any, and whether in law payable by/chargeable to Borrower or Lender or any other person, shall be to the account of, borne and paid solely and entirely by the Borrower in addition to and over and above any payments/reimbursements (including the Taxes on reimbursements, if any) to the Lender and accordingly all the payments/reimbursements to the Lender hereunder shall be exclusive of any Taxes.

The Borrower agrees that such credit balance in the Borrower’s account shall not attract any interest.

3.14 In case of any Default by the Borrower in payment of the Outstanding Balance on respective Due Dates or default of any of the other terms and conditions herein including on the occurrence of an Event of Default, the Borrower shall be liable to pay additional interest at the rate mentioned in the Schedule hereunder (“Overdue Interest”) on the Outstanding Balance, over and above the applicable Interest Rate from the date of the default till the date of actual payment in full or cure of default to the satisfaction of the Lender, as the case maybe, without prejudice to the Lender's other rights and remedies available whether under contract or any applicable law.

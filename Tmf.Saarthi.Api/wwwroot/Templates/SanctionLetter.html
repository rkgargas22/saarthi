LOAN DETAILS
Type of Loan: Fuel Loan
Purpose: Purchase of Fuel and Lubricants
Billing Cycle: 1st calendar day of a month and shall end on the last date of that month.
Billing Date: 1st calendar day or such other day, as may be decided by the Lender, of the month succeeding the Billing Cycle.
Limit Validity: 12 months from date of execution of the Loan Agreement
Monthly Statement: A monthly statement (either in physical or digital/electronic format) published/ issued by the Lender to the Borrower on a Billing Date, which consists of information pertaining to the tranches availed of by the Borrower, payable amount, Billing Cycle, Payment Due Date, the loan amount, principal amount, interest rate, other charges, status of the loan account of the Borrower etc.
Number of Drawdowns: Multiple drawdowns (in multiples of thousand) within the total limit
Payable Amount: The total amount (including principal, Interest and other monies/charges) payable by the Borrower to the Lender as reflected in the Monthly Statement on the Payment Due Date.
Payment Due Date: The 15th calendar day of the month in which the Monthly Statement was issued by the Lender to the Borrower.
Interest Rate: 18% Interest would be computed on daily running balance.
Accelerated Interest 24% (Applicable if the Payable Amount is not paid within the Payment Due Date)
Renewal of Facility: This loan shall be valid for a period of 365 (Three Hundred and Sixty Five) days and at the written request of the Borrower, can be renewed for such further period of 12 (twelve) months each. The decision for such renewal shall be at the sole discretion of the Lender.
Sanction letter validity: 30 (Thirty days)

Disbursement:
1. Customer can make drawdown request through TMFSL platform or selected OMC (Oil Marketing Companies) platform.
2. The Customer shall be assigned a central customer account by the OMC, which shall be tagged to the respective OMC customer ID. Any successful drawdown request shall lead to the recharge of the central customer account. All disbursements shall be made to such central customer account of the Customer as may be assigned by the respective OMC to the Customer and no disbursements shall be made into the bank account of the Customer.
3. Once the drawdown request is raised to the Lender by the Borrower or the Oil Company on behalf of the Borrower or any other person who may be so authorised, if the Loan amount so requested is within the Cut-off Limit (each, a Tranche), the amount of the Tranche shall be transferred by the Lender into the designated account of the Oil Company, and the Oil Company, in turn, shall then load the Tranche amount in to the central customer account of the Borrower.
4. Acceptance of drawdown request is subjected to the availability limit and account status of the Borrower. If drawdown request is not accepted due to any valid reason, such drawdown request shall be considered as unsuccessful drawdown request attempt.
Repayment Details:
1. The Borrower shall be liable to make the payment to the Lender of the Payable Amount on or before the Payment Due Date.
2. In the event if the Borrower fails to make the due and complete repayment to the Lender of the Payable Amount on or before the Payment Due Date, the Borrower shall be liable to pay Accelerated Interest on the outstanding balance (as defined in the Agreements) and such Accelerated Interest will continue to be charged by the Lender until the entire Payable Amount is repaid by the Borrower.
3. Repayment can be made through online transfer or by visiting any nearest branch for cash collection or Cheque/DD submission.
4. If the Customer fails to pay Payable Amount in full by the 29th calendar day of the month in which the Payment Due Date falls, the account would get frozen and the customer shall not be able to make any further drawdowns. Upon the customer making the full payment of the last Payable Amount, the account will revert back to Active status.
5. If the Customer’s account remains frozen for more than 90 (ninety) days, then such Customer’s account will be permanently blocked and loan recall procedures shall be initiated.
6. Facility termination request by the Customer shall be honored only if the Customer has cleared all the previous outstanding balance and doesn’t have any outstanding balance payable to TMFSL.
7. Any amount which is paid by the Borrower in excess of the Payable Amount for a particular month shall be adjusted against the Payable Amount for subsequent months,arising out of the Borrower’s future drawdown requests. The Borrower agrees that such credit balance in the Borrower’s account shall not attract any interest.
